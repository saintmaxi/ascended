Kristin Weber, violin

X

Since graduating with a degree in music from Berklee College of Music and moving to Nashville, Kristin Weber has toured world-wide playing with major country and pop artists as well as bluegrass and indie rock bands. She's written string arrangements and recorded on numerous records as a fiddler, classical player, and singer, including Dolly Parton, Margo Price, Midland, Cage the Elephant and Roy Orbison. 

She's performed with Lorde, Eminem, Kacey Musgraves, Lee Ann Womack, Cage The Elephant, Trampled By Turtles, Augustana, Michael Bublè, and she has appeared on Jimmy Fallon, Seth Meyers, Good Morning America, The Grand Ole Opry, and the CMT Awards.

Bobby Chase, violin

X

Bobby Chase is a violinist, violist, producer and composer based in Nashville, TN. His eclectic musical upbringing in Washington, DC led him to Nashville to pursue music production and performance professionally. Having graduated from Belmont University with a degree in Audio Engineering and Violin Performance, he continues to push himself musically and expand his horizons by staying active classically and commercially—playing in symphony orchestras, collaborating and recording with pop and indie artists, and arranging and composing works for everything from film scores to hip hop tracks. He recently traveled to Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, and Turkmenistan as a musical Ambassador for the “American Music Abroad” program, where he was able to learn from, teach, collaborate, and perform with local musicians and songwriters.

Bobby has been honored and humbled to have performed live or recorded in the studio with Ms. Lauryn Hill, Kacey Musgraves, Maren Morris, Martina McBride, Dan + Shay, Gillian Welch, Dave Rawlings, Nick Jonas, and many more.

Nicole Neely, viola

X

Nicole is a graduate of the University of South Carolina where she earned a Bachelor's degree in Music Education. She worked as an educator both on the K-12 and collegiate level. She eventually began working for the industry in 2008, moving to Nashville in December of 2017 to pursue a career as a composer/arranger.

Nicole has written and recorded arrangements for various artists such as Ms. Lauryn Hill, Eminem, Rihanna, Fantasia, T.I.'s Tiny Desk Performance on NPR, and H.E.R.'s 2018 BET Awards Performance along with Sony, Universal, and Motown artists. She has also performed with Michael Bublé, Eminem at Bonnaroo, Florida Georgia Line & Bebe Rexha on the CMA & Dove Awards.

Melodie Chase, cello

X

Melodie began playing cello in West Hartford, CT and made her way to Nashville to pursue music in 2009. She studied classically with Hartford Symphony and Nashville Symphony players and pushed the limits of her cello with some of Nashville’s best violinists, cellists, and bassists while at Belmont University.

She has recorded and performed with hundreds of artists, some favorites including Brandi Carlile, Trace Adkins, Coheed & Cambria, Steven Curtis Chapman, and Fletcher. Career highlights: Bonnaroo with Cage the Elephant, CMAs with Florida Georgia Line & Bebe Rexha, CMT Awards with Eric Church, and NBC’s “Smash” with Katharine McPhee and Jeremy Jordan.
